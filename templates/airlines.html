Understanding Your Rights with Different Airlines

Each airline has specific policies for handling flight disruptions, but regulations like EC 261/2004 provide standardized rights for passengers flying with EU-based airlines or from an EU airport. Compensation amounts and eligibility criteria can vary based on the airline and the specific circumstances of your flight disruption.
